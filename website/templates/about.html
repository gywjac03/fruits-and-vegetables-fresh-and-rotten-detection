About FreshScan

About Us

FreshScan is a AI-powered fruit freshness detection system developed to reduce food waste and promote sustainable consumption practices.

Our team combines expertise in artificial intelligence, computer vision, and food science to create innovative solutions for everyday problems. We believe that technology should be accessible and beneficial to everyone, which is why we've developed FreshScan to be user-friendly and practical.

With FreshScan, we're not just building a product – we're creating a movement towards smarter, more sustainable food consumption habits.

What We Are Trying to Solve

The Food Waste Crisis

  • Approximately 1.3 billion tons of food is wasted globally each year

  • Fruits and vegetables account for a significant portion of food waste

  • Consumers often struggle to determine fruit freshness accurately

Current Challenges

  • Traditional methods of checking fruit freshness are unreliable

  • Lack of accurate information leads to premature disposal

  • Difficulty in planning fruit consumption effectively

What Is FreshScan?

AI-Powered Analysis

Advanced machine learning algorithms analyze fruit images to determine freshness with high accuracy

Easy to Use

Simple point-and-scan interface makes it accessible to everyone, anywhere, anytime

Sustainable Impact

Help reduce food waste and promote sustainable consumption practices

FreshScan is more than just a tool – it's a step towards a more sustainable future. By helping users make informed decisions about their fruit consumption, we're contributing to the global effort to reduce food waste and promote sustainable living practices.
