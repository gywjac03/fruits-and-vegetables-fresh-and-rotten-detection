How FreshScan Works

1

Take a Photo

Simply point your camera at any fruit and take a clear photo. FreshScan works best with good lighting and a focused image. You can take photos of individual fruits or groups of the same type.

2

AI Analysis

Our advanced AI algorithms analyze the uploaded image or image in real-time, identifying the fruit type and assessing its freshness.

3

Freshness Assessment

FreshScan provides a detailed freshness assessment. The system provides an estimated shelf life and storage recommendations to ensure the fruit lasts as long as possible.

4

Storage Recommendations

Get personalized storage tips based on the fruit type. FreshScan recommends optimal storage conditions to extend the shelf life of your fruits.

5

Track and Manage

Save your scan results to track freshness over time. Track when the fruits are approaching their expiration date and manage your fruit inventory efficiently.
